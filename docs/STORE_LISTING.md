## Short Description

Recurring memo reminders for Android.

## Full Description

DigDone helps you create simple recurring memo reminders that stay on your device.

Create a memo, choose the first reminder date and time, and select a repeat schedule: hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. DigDone sends a notification when the memo is due and schedules the next reminder automatically.

Features:

- Create recurring memo reminders
- Repeat hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly
- Store memos locally on your device
- Reschedule reminders after reboot
- Disable notifications with a phrase-based setting
- No account required
- No analytics or advertising SDKs

DigDone is designed for lightweight personal reminders where your notes remain local to your Android device.
