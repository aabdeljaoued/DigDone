# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

DigDone is a recurring memo reminder app for Android.

## Data Collection

DigDone does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Local Data Storage

DigDone stores memo titles, memo notes, recurrence settings, reminder times, and notification settings locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data stays on your device unless you choose to back up or transfer your device data through Android or another service.

## Notifications

DigDone uses Android notifications to remind you when a memo is due. Notification permission is requested on Android versions that require it.

## Reminders

DigDone uses Android alarm scheduling to trigger local reminders. Android may adjust delivery time for battery optimization.

## Third Parties

DigDone does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or backend services.

## Data Deletion

You can delete memos in the app. You can also remove all app data by uninstalling DigDone or clearing app storage from Android settings.
